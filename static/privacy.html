Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sorame ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our URL shortening service.

Information We Collect

When you use Sorame, we collect the following information:

  • The URL you wish to shorten
  • Basic access logs including IP address and user agent
  • Time and date of your visit

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Monitor usage patterns and traffic
  • Prevent abuse of our service
  • Improve our service

Data Retention

URLs that you shorten are stored in our database for 30 days, after which they automatically expire and are deleted from our system.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We may share aggregated, non-personal information with third parties for analytics purposes.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
